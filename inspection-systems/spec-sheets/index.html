Before the PDF

Start with the fit question.

Spec sheets are useful once the inspection goal is clear. Use these fit checks to decide which brochures to review first.

01

Product and package size

Compare the inspection opening and usable belt width against the largest expected product and package.

02

Line speed and handling

Review conveyor size and published max line speed, then confirm product spacing, orientation, and reject handling.

03

Environment and sanitation

Use IP66 or IP69K configuration as an initial sanitation fit screen. Confirm washdown and plant requirements during review.
