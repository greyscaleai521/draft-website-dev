Protein

Inspection intelligence for protein production.

GreyscaleAI helps protein producers detect foreign material, monitor inspection activity, understand product quality, and see what is happening across lines and plants.

Production challenges

Protein inspection needs more context than a reject bin can provide.

Applications can include chicken nuggets, beef jerky, formed meats, sausage, frozen protein, and prepared protein products. Each product and package should be reviewed for the specific inspection goal, line speed, and application conditions.

  • Foreign material detection
    Support QA and food safety workflows with x-ray inspection and event review.
  • Product quality visibility
    Understand density, shape, missing product, breakage, or other attributes where accurate for the application.
  • Machine monitoring
    Give plant and maintenance teams visibility into system health and inspection activity.
