Pet Food

Inspection intelligence for pet food production.

GreyscaleAI supports pet food producers with x-ray inspection, image analysis, machine monitoring, and Insights visibility.

Inspection needs

Bring inspection context into QA and operations.

Pet food lines can span kibble, treats, raw formats, and mixed-pack products, each with different density, packaging, and traceability requirements. Teams often need both contaminant review and better access to event history.

  • Foreign material detection
    Review product format, packaging, density, and contaminant concerns.
  • Image history
    Preserve inspection events for review and documentation workflows.
  • Support model
    Use monitoring and application support through the standard service agreement.

Common pet food formats

  • Kibble
  • Treats
  • Frozen raw products
  • Patty formats
  • Mixed packaged products

Typical inspection concerns

  • Metal, glass, or dense plastic contamination
  • Traceable image history for customer or QA review
  • Count or fill variation in packaged formats
  • Operational visibility across multiple lines

Common questions

Questions pet-food teams often raise.

Can one system handle multiple pet food formats?

Sometimes, but it depends on how different the products and packages are. Mixed product families often need application-specific tuning or separate validated workflows.

Can we keep image history for investigations and customer follow-up?

Yes. Searchable image history and event metadata can support QA documentation, internal review, and recurring issue analysis.

Does the support model include monitoring?

Yes. Machine monitoring and application support are part of the standard service approach so troubleshooting starts with system context.
