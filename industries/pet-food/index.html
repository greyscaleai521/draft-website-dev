PET FOOD

Inspection intelligence built around the pet food format.

From dry kibble and treats to wet, fresh, and frozen products, GreyscaleAI connects high-resolution x-ray inspection, product-specific AI, and Insights software so teams can review foreign material events, quality signals, and production patterns with image evidence.

Talk Through Fit Explore Pet Food Applications

PRODUCTION CHALLENGES

One category. Very different inspection conditions.

Pet food lines can move from uniform dry pieces to dense wet products, raw or frozen patties, and mixed-component packs. Recipe, moisture, thickness, inclusions, package material, and line speed all affect what the x-ray image can show and how the application should be validated.

Safety signals

Review dense foreign material events with the inspection image and production context attached.

Normal product variation

Support review of expected recipe and density variation versus signals that merit QA attention.

Quality and package checks

Evaluate count, presence, breakage, fill distribution, or package-related conditions where the product and image source support validation.

Multiple products and lines

Use consistent event history and filters to compare activity across products, shifts, machines, and facilities.

Application fit is product-specific. Detection and classification performance depends on the recipe, product thickness, moisture, package, target condition, line speed, and validation set.
