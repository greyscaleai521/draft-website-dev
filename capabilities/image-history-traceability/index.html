Capability

Image History & Traceability

Store inspection images, event metadata, and machine context so QA and operations teams can search, review, and document what happened.

Retention and review

Keep the evidence, not just the outcome.

Images and events are stored for agreed retention periods based on the customer deployment. Teams can search by time, SKU, line, event type, and related metadata to support investigations, traceability requests, and recurring quality review.

  • Retention policy
    Image and event retention is defined with the customer rather than assumed as a fixed generic setting.
  • Searchable metadata
    Time, product, line, event type, and machine details help narrow the record quickly.
  • Controlled access
    Authorized users can export, review, or delete records according to the agreed workflow.
