Product Quality Inspection

The same X-ray images used for inspection can also surface quality signals such as voids, clumps, missing components, density variation, and broken pieces.

Quality signals

See product conditions that operators and QA teams often miss at line speed.

GreyscaleAI uses high-resolution images and AI models to classify product-specific conditions that relate to fill, distribution, structure, count, and breakage. The useful signals vary by product, package, and inspection objective.

  • Voids and underfill
    Spot missing product mass or empty spaces inside formed items.
  • Clumps and distribution
    Detect uneven ingredient spread or density pockets inside packaged food.
  • Missing components
    Count expected pieces or confirm that required inclusions are present.
  • Broken product
    Flag fractures, collapsed structure, or shape loss that matters to quality grading.
