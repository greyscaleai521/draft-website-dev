Capability

Production Analytics

Inspection activity becomes more valuable when teams can see reject trends, recurring reasons, and relationships between quality outcomes and production conditions.

Dashboard outcomes

Turn machine-speed activity into operational visibility.

Production analytics helps QA, plant management, and corporate operations move from isolated events to recurring patterns. Instead of reacting to one reject, teams can compare lines, shifts, products, and suppliers using common metrics.

These analytics are surfaced in GreyscaleAI Insights so authorized QA, plant management, and corporate operations teams can review inspection activity from a shared application layer.

  • Reject rate over time
    See when events increase and whether the change is isolated or recurring.
  • Top reasons for rejects
    Rank the most common conditions driving line loss or review effort.
  • Shift and line correlation
    Compare quality patterns against shift, line, SKU, or supplier context.

Example dashboards

Show the metrics teams actually use.

HOW TEAMS USE IT

Analytics are most useful when they lead to action.

Supplier review

Use recurring reject patterns to identify whether quality changes are tied to supplier, lot, or SKU.

Shift coaching

Compare inspection outcomes by shift so training and operating practices can be reviewed with evidence.

Maintenance prioritization

Connect quality or reject trends with machine health signals to decide whether an issue needs maintenance review.

Process adjustment

Use trends to evaluate whether line speed, recipe, temperature, or presentation changes are affecting inspection outcomes.
