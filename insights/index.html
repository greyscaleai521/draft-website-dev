GreyscaleAI Insights

The application for seeing what your inspection systems are telling you across machines, lines, and plants.

Beyond Rejects

Inspection data should do more than trigger a reject.

Insights gives authorized users visibility into inspection systems across machines, lines, and plants without requiring them to be in front of the equipment. Through Beyond Rejects, Insights turns inspection activity into meaningful alerts, trends, and operational visibility.

Instead of only knowing that a product was rejected, teams can understand what is changing, where issues are appearing, and when action may be needed.

Visibility across inspection activity.
