PLATFORM

Inspection intelligence for food production.

GreyscaleAI connects HRX inspection systems, AI image analysis, GreyscaleAI Insights software, machine visibility, alerts, and managed support into one platform for food production teams.

Explore Applications See Insights Software
X-ray inspection AI image analysis Cloud visibility

WHY PLATFORM

Inspection should not stop at pass or fail.

Traditional inspection systems can reject product, but they often leave teams without enough context to understand what happened, whether the issue is isolated, or what pattern is developing across a line, shift, product, or plant.

Traditional inspection

  • Rejects product, but stores limited review context.
  • Makes QA rely on screenshots, notes, or manual follow-up.
  • Separates machine status from inspection history.
  • Makes trend review difficult across lines or plants.

GreyscaleAI platform

  • Captures X-ray image evidence at line speed.
  • Uses AI to classify product-specific signals.
  • Stores images, events, metadata, alerts, and machine context in Insights.
  • Gives QA, operations, maintenance, and support a shared operating record.

HOW IT WORKS

Inspect. Analyze. Connect. Alert. Improve.

The platform loop starts with every inspected product and ends with evidence, trends, alerts, and support context that teams can use.

01

Inspect

HRX systems capture high-resolution X-ray images of product flow at line speed.

02

Analyze

AI models evaluate image evidence for foreign material, quality, count, density, shape, or other product-specific signals.

03

Connect

Images, AI decisions, event metadata, product context, and machine context are stored in GreyscaleAI Insights.

04

Alert

Meaningful alerts surface quality, reject-rate, machine, or trend changes with supporting context.

05

Improve

Monitoring, support, updates, analytics, and application learning help the system become more useful over time.

WHO USES IT

A shared operating record for the teams around the line.

The value of a connected platform is that different teams can work from the same image evidence, event history, alerts, machine context, and trend data instead of separate notes, screenshots, or assumptions.

FSQA

Review inspection evidence, investigate events, document follow-up, support traceability workflows, and decide when action is needed.

Operations

See patterns by product, line, shift, plant, supplier, or time window so recurring issues are easier to recognize and prioritize.

Maintenance

Review machine status, health signals, alerts, remote screen context, and recent activity before troubleshooting begins.

Enterprise teams

Give authorized users secure access to inspection and machine context across connected facilities without adding another local system to maintain.
