HRX systems

Choose the right inspection configuration for the application.

Key specs below are pulled from the published January 2026 GreyscaleAI brochures. Typical applications are directional fit notes for buyers, not hard limits; detailed validation should still be reviewed against the actual product, package, and line conditions.

Inspection system

HRX660

Standard HRX inspection system for larger-format food applications that need high-resolution x-ray inspection and platform connectivity.

  • Inspection opening: 639 x 254 mm
  • Conveyor: 60 x 30 in
  • Throughput: up to 60 m/min line speed, product-dependent
  • Typical applications: protein, dairy blocks, packaged frozen foods, large-format products
Download PDF

Inspection system

HRX508AQ

HRX configuration for automated quality checks, image analysis, and larger-format product inspection workflows.

  • Inspection opening: 452 x 175 mm
  • Conveyor: 60 x 30 in
  • Throughput: up to 60 m/min line speed, product-dependent
  • Typical applications: tray-packed proteins, cheese and dairy formats, frozen packaged foods, high-noise products
Download PDF

Inspection system

HRX660AQ

Automated inspection configuration connected to Insights, monitoring, alerts, and grading-oriented workflows.

  • Inspection opening: 596 x 225 mm
  • Conveyor: 60 x 30 in
  • Throughput: up to 60 m/min line speed, product-dependent
  • Typical applications: protein, dairy blocks, packaged frozen foods, weight intelligence and quality workflows
Download PDF

Inspection system

HRX380AQ

Compact HRX configuration for product-specific inspection, grading, and quality-visibility workflows.

  • Inspection opening: 307 x 100 mm
  • Conveyor: compact conveyor, 307 mm usable inspection width
  • Throughput: up to 80 m/min line speed, product-dependent
  • Typical applications: cheese, packaged goods, component verification, seal-related inspection workflows
Download PDF

Inspection system

HRX380

Standard compact HRX machine option for line-side x-ray inspection, image capture, and smaller-footprint lines.

  • Inspection opening: 329 x 102 mm
  • Conveyor: conveyor included, 329 mm usable inspection width
  • Throughput: up to 80 m/min line speed, product-dependent
  • Typical applications: packaged foods, cheese, compact line installations, seal and inclusion inspection
