Platform overview

Inspection intelligence platform for food production.

GreyscaleAI connects standard HRX inspection systems, AI image analysis, GreyscaleAI Insights, machine monitoring, and managed support into one product platform for production teams.

Architecture

Machines plus AI plus Insights plus support.

MachinesStandard HRX inspection systems and configurations for line-side inspection.
X-ray imageHigh-resolution inspection images create a reusable production data source.
AI analysisModels help identify foreign material, quality variation, grading, checkweighing, and product attributes.
InsightsAuthorized users see machine visibility, alerts, trends, image history, and multi-plant inspection intelligence.
Monitoring & supportInspection as a Service keeps systems monitored, supported, updated, and improving.

Inspect layer

High-resolution x-ray inspection at machine speed.

GreyscaleAI inspection systems are built for food production environments where product format, packaging, line speed, and contaminant risk all matter.

  • Foreign material detection
    Support contaminant detection workflows with careful application review.
  • Product quality inspection
    Use image data to support product-specific quality checks where appropriate.
  • Checkweighing and grading
    Combine inspection functions into a practical line-side workflow.
  • Image history
    Preserve inspection context for event review and traceability workflows.

Analyze layer

AI image analysis turns inspection images into useful signals.

The platform analyzes x-ray images to help teams understand product, package, quality, and inspection events beyond a simple reject count.

Detection performance depends on product, packaging, contaminant type, size, density, and application conditions.
