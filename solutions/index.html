Platform overview

Inspection intelligence platform for food production.

GreyscaleAI connects standard HRX inspection systems, AI image analysis, GreyscaleAI Insights, machine monitoring, and managed support into one product platform for production teams.

Architecture

Inspection systems plus AI plus Insights plus support.

GreyscaleAI connects standard HRX systems with AI image analysis, Insights software, and a managed support model.

1 Inspection machine

Standard HRX systems capture every product at line speed.

2 AI layer

X-ray images feed AI models that assess product-specific inspection signals.

3 Insights software

Images, events, trends, and alerts stay available to authorized users.

4 Support layer

Monitoring, updates, and application support keep the platform improving over time.

Inspect layer

High-resolution x-ray inspection at machine speed.

GreyscaleAI inspection systems are built for food production environments where product format, packaging, line speed, and contaminant risk all matter.

  • Foreign material detection
    High-resolution X-ray and AI models help detect metal, bone, glass, or other dense contaminants. Detection performance depends on your product, packaging, and contaminant type.
  • Product quality inspection
    Assess density, shape, fill, count, or other product-specific attributes. Accuracy depends on your product format, packaging, and inspection conditions.
  • Checkweighing and grading
    Combine inspection functions into a practical line-side workflow. Performance depends on your product, packaging, and application goals.
  • Image history
    Preserve inspection context for event review and traceability workflows. Usability depends on your image quality, line setup, and retention policy.

Analyze layer

AI image analysis turns inspection images into useful signals.

AI models classify defects, measure density and shape, count missing components, and identify quality bands. These signals feed into grading, checkweighing, and analytics.

Security & access

Built for customer IT stakeholders.

Authorized users

Access is framed around approved users across the customer organization, not uncontrolled broad access.

Single sign-on

Insights integrates with customer authentication so teams do not need another separate login.

No added local maintenance burden

GreyscaleAI manages the application layer so IT teams do not maintain another local application.

Retention and privacy

Images and data are stored securely for agreed retention periods, with customer control over export and deletion.
